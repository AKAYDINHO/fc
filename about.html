About Us

Welcome to our Football Club. Established in 1901, we are one of the oldest and most successful clubs in the league. We pride ourselves on our history, our fans, and our future. Our club has won numerous championships and continues to strive for excellence both on and off the field.

Our mission is to foster a community of passionate football enthusiasts and to nurture young talents to reach their full potential. We are committed to promoting the spirit of sportsmanship and teamwork.

Our home ground, the iconic Soccer City Stadium, has been a fortress for our team and a venue for many memorable matches. We invite you to join us in celebrating the beautiful game.
